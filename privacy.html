Privacy Policy

How Rescue Haven collects, uses, and protects your information.

Quick note: This is a general template. Please have a legal professional review and tailor it for your organization and local regulations.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you submit an adoption application, volunteer form, donation, or contact request. This may include your name, contact details, address, and information about your household and pets.

How we use your information

We use your information to process applications, communicate with you about animals and events, issue donation receipts, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing your information

We may share your information with trusted service providers (for example, payment processors or email services) solely to perform services on our behalf, or as required by law.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please contact us if you have any concerns about your data.

Your choices

You may request to access, update, or delete certain personal information we hold about you, subject to legal or record‑keeping requirements.
